PLANNING 2050

DASHBOARD | Reporting progress

Through this platform all signatories of the 2050 Commitment will be able to report, monitor, adjust, and collaborate to achieve impactful change thru actionable planning.

With PLANNING2050, cities and communities will ensure critical systems are resilient, carbon neutral, and equitable by 2050.

About the Planning2050 initiative

SUPPORTIVE organizations

Systems Thinking & Collaboration

PLANNING2050 is a multidisciplinary effort supported by various professional organizations. As a systems thinking approach overlaps diverse perspectives, PLANNING2050 successfully brings together various disciplines to help solving important problems.

Founded : 2021

Supportive Professional Organizations:

APA SCD (American Planning Association -Sustainable Communities Division), AIA (American Institute of Architects), CNU (The Congress for new urbanism), ISI (Institute for Sustainable Infrastructure), USGBC (United States Green Building Council), ISOCARP (International Society of City and Regional Planners), CAP (Commonwealth Association of Planners)

Disciplines involved: Planning, Architecture, Urban Design, Civil Engineering, Education, and more...
