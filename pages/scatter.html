Dashboard | Mapping your Strategy

Welcome. If this is the first time you are "mapping" your strategy, please click on the canvas where you think your strategy or action in your 2050Commitment is located today. When clicking, you will be drawing a dot and be prompted a few questions.

There is no right or wrong answer here. Your picked location should be based on your current interpretation of how much IMPACT ("X" axis), and how COMMON ("Y" axis) is your solution / action.

When mapping this, think about your city or community, compared to what you consider peer cities / communities

1. Click on the canvas as many times as you want. Recommended: 3 dots at a time. You can add more strategies later (if needed)

2. Name your Strategy when prompted

3. Click on "Manage My Strategies" button to add details for each dot
